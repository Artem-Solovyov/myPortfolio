However, out of curiosity, I began to learn Python, and after a week I realized that in my life I had not yet received so much pleasure as from learning programming languages. After some time, I decided on the direction and moved on to an in-depth study of the css, html and js.

Now I already have half a year of commercial and a lot of practical experience behind me. I do not stand still and study new technologies and libraries every day. I have a huge desire to create something cool and new.

At this stage, my dream is to start creating cool projects on the React library. However, this alone is not entirely realistic. But my journey in IT has just begun and there is still a lot of things ahead.

And that's what I can say for sure - I'm madly in love with my work and when you turn to me you get a quality product.
