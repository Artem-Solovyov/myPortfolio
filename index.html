About me

I am a website developer based in Kyiv, Ukraine, and I excel at what I do. My journey has been quite remarkable. Just two years ago, I was working in the construction industry and content with my life. However, driven by curiosity, I embarked on learning Python. Within a week, I realized that nothing had brought me as much joy as delving into programming languages.

Over time, I made a decision and transitioned to a comprehensive study of CSS, HTML, and JavaScript. Today, I boast six months of commercial experience and a wealth of practical knowledge. I refuse to stand still and make it a daily practice to explore new technologies and libraries. I possess an immense passion for creating innovative and impressive projects.

My current aspiration is to embark on creating exceptional projects using the React library. While it may seem ambitious, I acknowledge that my journey in the field of IT has only just begun, and there are countless opportunities lying ahead.

One thing I can assure you without a doubt is that I am deeply passionate about my work. When you entrust your projects to me, you can expect nothing less than a top-quality product.
